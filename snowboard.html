🎒 What to Pack:

  • Snow Gear: Snowboard/skis, boots, bindings (if you have them), helmet
  • Warm Clothes: Base layers, fleece, waterproof jacket and pants, warm socks, gloves/mittens, hat, neck gaiter.
  • Eye Protection: Goggles or sunglasses.
  • Sunscreen: The sun is still strong on the snow. ☀️
  • Swimsuit: In case of a hot tub! 🛁
  • Toiletries & Personal Items: Don’t forget them!
  • First Aid Kit: Band-aids, pain reliever, etc.
  • Snacks: Save some money with your favorite food.

📍 Blue Mountain Highlights:

  • Variety of Terrain: Something for all skill levels. 🟢🟡⚫
  • Summit Views: Enjoy the scenery from the top! ⛰️
  • Snow Tubing: A fun alternative activity if you need a break from the slopes. 🛟
  • Après-Ski: Check out local spots for drinks and food. 🍻

✅ Important Reminders:

  • Book Everything in Advance: Secure your lift tickets, rentals, and Airbnb ASAP!
  • Check Snow Conditions: Make sure there’s plenty of snow before you go. ❄️
  • Be Safe & Have Fun! Ski or snowboard within your abilities. ⚠️
